About Archesian

A Kampala practice for integrated building work.

Archesian works across architecture, engineering, quantity surveying, project management, design-build delivery, and renovations.

Approach

One team thinking across drawings, cost, contracts, and site.

The strength of Archesian is vertical integration. Architecture sets the spatial and technical intent. Engineering tests buildability. Quantity surveying keeps cost decisions visible. Project management protects sequence, communication, and delivery.

That structure helps clients make earlier decisions with fewer handoffs between drawings, BOQs, procurement, supervision, and site reporting.
