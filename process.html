Process

A clear route from brief to handover.

The process is designed to keep drawings, cost, procurement, and site decisions visible before they become delays.

01

Brief

Client consultation, requirements gathering, site constraints, approvals path, budget frame, and decision criteria.

Typical output: project brief

02

Design

Concept, schematic design, technical drawings, specifications, consultant input, and approvals support.

Typical output: drawing set

03

Plan

BOQ, procurement sequencing, work programme, contract inputs, cash flow, and value engineering.

Typical output: BOQ + schedule

04

Build

Construction, supervision, consultant coordination, site reporting, quality control, and cost tracking.

Typical output: progress reports

05

Handover

Snagging, commissioning, documentation, final accounts, and post-occupancy support where required.

Typical output: handover pack
